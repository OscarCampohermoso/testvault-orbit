# Privacy Policy for TestVault Orbit

Effective Date: March 26, 2026

TestVault Orbit is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle data within the extension.

### 1. Data Collection and Usage
TestVault Orbit is a local-first extension. All credentials, tags, and domain patterns you save are stored locally within your browser's storage (Chrome Storage API). We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to external servers.

### 2. Permissions Justification
- **Authentication Information:** The extension stores test credentials (usernames/passwords) solely to facilitate the autofill feature on user-defined environments.
- **Website Content:** The extension interacts with the webpage's DOM only to identify login fields and perform the autofill action upon user request.
- **Storage:** Used to save your account vault locally in your browser profile.

### 3. Data Sharing and Third Parties
We do not sell, trade, or transfer any user data to third parties. There is no remote tracking or analytics integrated into the extension.

### 4. User Control
All data remains under the user's control. You can view, edit, or delete your stored credentials at any time through the extension interface. Clearing your browser data or uninstalling the extension will remove all stored local data.

### 5. Contact
If you have any questions, you can contact the developer through the official GitHub repository: https://github.com/OscarCampohermoso/testvault-orbit
